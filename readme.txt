Welcome to Craving Cake,

A flavourful bakery, where we deliver; homemade cakes, cupcakes, sweet treats, afternoon tea, as well as perfect gifts to your door. Further information can be found on our appetising website!  Craving Cake is very well-known for its delicious sweet treats, baked goods, and cakes. The magic happens in our family's home using special baking equipment, which you can also buy from our Amazon store!
Our story began during lockdown, when we, and probably you, had a lot of free time! To keep ourselves sane, we began baking for friends and family, which quickly grew and necessitated the formation of a small family team of helpers.
Since then, we've built our own Amazon store to supplement the main baking side of the business. We are grateful to our customers for their support of our baking dream, and we hope to open our own small shop in the coming months. Exciting!
